Who we are

This website is operated by Arkminds AB, a Swedish company registered in Gothenburg. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at kristian@arkminds.se.

What data we collect

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage statistics. This helps us understand how visitors interact with the site. The data collected includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or traffic source
  • Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Approximate geographic location (city-level, derived from IP address)
  • Whether you are a new or returning visitor

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information through analytics. All data is anonymous.

Cookies used

Google Analytics sets the following first-party cookies on your device, only after you give consent:

CookiePurposeDuration
_ga Distinguishes unique visitors using a randomly generated ID 2 years
_ga_KDVBWY77R0 Stores session state (session count and start time) 2 years

We use Cookiebot as our consent management platform. Cookiebot sets a cookie (CookieConsent) to remember your consent choice. This cookie is classified as necessary and does not require consent itself.

Legal basis

We process analytics data based on your consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). Analytics cookies are only set after you actively click "Accept" on the consent banner. If you decline, no tracking cookies are set and no identifying data is collected.

Data processor

Analytics data is processed by Google LLC (Google Analytics 4). Google may transfer data to the United States under the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time by clicking "Cookie settings" in the site footer, or by clearing your browser's cookies.
  • Access any personal data we hold about you (in practice, we hold none that is personally identifiable).
  • Request erasure of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY) at imy.se.
